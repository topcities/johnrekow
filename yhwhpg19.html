Self-forgiveness



Self-forgiveness is the concept of you forgiving yourself.
The Bible does not really go into this perspective. It talks about God forgiving you first through Jesus Christ,
and that you are to forgive others.
If you consider the idea that the "others" could be or could include your own self, then here is a collection of verses that can help you forgive yourself, if you think about the "one another" and the "others" including your own self.
Just remember that the first priority is for you to be saved and forgiven by God, and then the second priority is that we need to forgive others so that we will also be forgiven. We have talked about these first two priorities many times before. You should pray about these first two priorities first, and then, if you need to pray about you forgiving yourself, these Bible verses can help with that.
May the forgiveness that we are studying about here bring healing to our own selves as well as healing into others.

Commentary: The album was released first, then the first single, No Longer Slaves, was not released until almost seven months later.
This is the opposite of the usual marketing strategy in the music industry.
Usually the single is released just before the album is released.
The single is a single song chosen and released for promotion, individual purchase, downloading, YouTube viewing, and radio play, to help promote the new, soon-to-be-released album, and generate some interest and excitement about a new album just before it gets released,
by releasing a single beforehand, to help stimulate promotion for the band and the new album.
Be that as it may, the album was released first in January 2015, and then the first single was not released until almost seven months later, in August.

"No Longer Slaves" is a song by Bethel Music featuring Jonathan David & Melissa Helser and it was released as a single on 21 August 2015 as Bethel Music's first, lead single from their seventh live album, We Will Not Be Shaken.

The song won the "Worship Song of the Year" award at the 47th Annual Gospel Music Association (GMA) Dove Awards in 2016,
