Ropes help you go up a cliff (into heaven) and they can help you not fall down the cliff (down into sin, death and hell).
Ropes help make bridges, and help those in the outdoors in many, many ways. Jesus is like having strong rope. Jesus gives you rest, He is the bridge to cross the great divide, because of the cross to bridge the great divide.
Many of the things that rope can do, can also be a picture of what Jesus does for us. Think about what rope is used for, and you'll be thinking about what Jesus does for us.

Get Rooted in the Word of God so that you can produce the Fruit of The Spirit.

Go deeper, and get deeply rooted in the Word of God.

What are the things that can help you go deeper, and what things could stop or block your ability to go deeper?
