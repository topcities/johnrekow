Here are some approximate ways to pronounce and spell some words and names in the ancient Hebrew language, using English letters.
Enoch is pronounced, EE-Knock. Enoch's name in Hebrew is pronounced, Haw-Nook.
Using English letters instead of Hebrew characters,
Enoch is spelled, E n o c h .
Again, Enoch's name in Hebrew is pronounced, Haw-Nook.
To spell Haw-Nook with English letters, it may end up being spelled like this.
Haw-Nook can be spelled, C h a n o k .
