Commentary:
The book of Psalms is a collection of songs that were written throughout many centuries.
The Psalms could have been written at least 650 years before Jesus Christ was born.
Some of the Psalms could have been written even as long ago as 1500 years before Jesus Christ was born.
With that in mind, Psalms 107 told what Jesus Christ was going to do, many, many centuries before He was even born.
Let's read this prophecy here in the Old Testament, and then see it come true in the New Testament.
There are many, many other prophecies about Jesus in the Old Testament that became true in the New Testament.
We saw a big collection of them in Isaiah chapters 52 and 53, in Christian Essays 8.
Here is yet one more prophecy about Jesus Christ...
