Commentary: Notice, in verse 1 of Psalms chapter 1, when sin and evil are creeping into your life, first you walk to it, then you spend time, standing around with it, then you sit down with it. First you walk, then you stand still, hanging around it, then you sit down and spend time in its company.

Be careful where you walk, where you stand, where you hang around, what you dabble in, and what you click on, or, before you know it, you'll be just like one of them, thinking the same way, saying the same things they say, and doing the same things they do.

Psalms 120-134 are fifteen Psalms that are known as Songs of the Ascents, Gradual Psalms, Songs of Degrees, Songs of Steps or Pilgrim Songs.
They were sung by worshippers as they ascended the road to Jerusalem to attend the three pilgrim festivals indicated in Deuteronomy 16:16.
Deuteronomy 16, verses 16 & 17 indicate that every man was required to go and do this, bringing a gift, and must give as they are able, according to the blessings given to them by the Lord your God.
Those three pilgrim festivals are when people, especially the men, would make a pilgrimage up to the Temple in Jerusalem.
The three pilgrim festivals are known as, Passover; the Festival of Harvest, Weeks or Pentecost; and the Festival of Tabernacles, Tents, Booths, or Shelters.

The Book of Enoch contains about 105 to 107 chapters. Different translators numbered some of the chapters slightly different, causing some chapter number locations to not be in agreement with different English translations.

Here is a short outline of the Book of Enoch.
The Book of Enoch has five main sections...
Section 1 of 5. Book of the Watchers (chapters 1-36) rewards, punishment, the end of the world and final judgement, angels, the Tree of Life, Jerusalem, and the universe
Section 2 of 5. Book of the Similitudes (chapters 37-71) Messiah, resurrection, the flood, Noah, future judgement, and paradise.
Section 3 of 5. Book of the Astronomical Writings (chapters 72-82) stars, moon, astronomy, the universe, and Enoch's mission
Section 4 of 5. Book of the Dream Visions (chapters 83-90) the flood, a historical sketch, the future of Israel, and the Messianic kingdom
Section 5 of 5. Book of the Epistle of Enoch (chapters 91-107) challenges for the wicked and righteous, the sin after the flood until the coming of the Messiah
