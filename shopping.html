FYI:   General information about Levodopa and Triphala...

Levodopa, or L-Dopa, is a naturally occurring amino acid and a precursor to dopamine, primarily used to treat Parkinson's disease and related movement disorders. It helps alleviate symptoms like tremors, stiffness, and bradykinesia by increasing dopamine levels in the brain. Nutritionally, it may support mood and cognitive function, but its primary health benefit lies in improving motor control and quality of life for individuals with Parkinson's disease.

Triphala Fruit Powder is a traditional Ayurvedic herbal blend made from three fruits: Amalaki, Bibhitaki, and Haritaki. It is known for its antioxidant properties and digestive health benefits, promoting regular bowel movements and detoxification. Nutritionally, it supports immune function, enhances metabolism, and may improve skin health. Triphala is primarily taken to aid digestive issues, constipation, and overall wellness, making it a popular supplement for holistic health.
