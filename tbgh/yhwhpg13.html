Who Am I In Christ?

In Christ I am...

CHOSEN BY GOD - "... He hath chosen us in Him before the foundation of the world, that we should be holy and without blame before Him in love." Eph 1:4

MADE PERFECT FOREVER - "For by one offering He hath perfected forever them that are sanctified." Heb 10:14

REDEEMED - "In whom we have redemption through His blood, the forgiveness of sins..." Colossians 1:14

MADE AT PEACE WITH GOD - "Therefore being justified by faith, we have peace with God through our Lord Jesus Christ." Rom 5:1

TOTALLY FORGIVEN - "And you, being dead in your sins and the uncircumcised of your flesh, hath He quickened together with Him, having forgiven you all trespasses;" Col 2:13

TOTALLY CLEANSED - "... but ye are washed, but ye are sanctified, but ye are justified in the name of the Lord Jesus, and by the Spirit of our God." 1 Cor 6:11

MADE HOLY AND BLAMELESS - "In the body of His flesh through death, to present you holy and unblameable and unreproveable in His sight." Col 1:22

GIVEN CHRIST'S RIGHTEOUSNESS - "For He hath made Him to be sin for us, who knew no sin; that we might be made the righteousness of God in Him." 2 Cor 5:21

FREED FROM CONDEMNATION - "There is therefore now no condemnation to them which are in Christ Jesus, who walk not after the flesh, but after the Spirit." Rom 8:1

MADE INTO A NEW CREATION - "Therefore if any man be in Christ, he is a new creature: old things are passed away; behold, all things are become new." 2 Cor 5:17

RECONCILED TO GOD - " For if, when we were enemies, we were reconciled to God by the death of His son much more, being reconciled, we shall be saved by His life." Rom 5:10

MADE HEIRS OF GOD - "Wherefore thou art no more a servant, but a son; and if a son, then an heir of God through Christ." Gal 4:7

HIDDEN WITH CHRIST IN GOD - "For ye are dead, and your life is hid with Christ in God." Col 3:3

IN FELLOWSHIP WITH GOD - "God is faithful, by whom ye were called unto the fellowship of His Son Jesus Christ our Lord." 1 Cor 1:9

GIVEN TOTAL ACCESS TO GOD - " In whom we have boldness and access with confidence by the faith of him." Eph 3:12

MADE COMPLETE - "For in Him dwelleth all the fulness of the Godhead bodily. And ye are complete in Him..." Col 2:9-10

GIVEN EVERYTHING - "According as His divine power hath given unto us all things that pertain unto life and godliness, through the knowledge of Him that hath called us to glory and virtue." 2 Pet 1:3

"How Great Thou Art" originally was an 1885 Christian hymn based on a
Swedish traditional melody and a poem, titled, O Great God,
written by Carl Boberg, and later Translated into English, and made into an English hymn by Stuart K. Hine in 1949.

The 1949 English hymn version became the best-loved hymn of the Billy Graham Crusades. They used it over and over again. In New York, in 1957, it was used more than 100 times by George Beverly Shea and the Crusade Choir in 119 meetings. In 1959, it became the theme of Billy Graham's "Hour of Decision" weekly radio broadcast.

Evangelist Billy Graham was once quoted as saying:
"The reason I like 'How Great Thou Art' is because it glorifies God. It turns Christian's eyes toward God, rather than upon themselves. I use it as often as possible because it is such a God-honouring song."

Lawrence Welk, who hosted the television programme, The Lawrence Welk Show, from 1951 to 1982, has been quoted as saying, "Of all the songs, both religious and secular, How Great Thou Art, was the most requested song for our television programme."

"Revelation Song" first gained exposure on U.S. Christian radio through a Christ for the Nations Institute 2004 live album, "Glorious," as well as on Gateway Worship's 2005 debut album, "Living for You," and subsequently included on Kari Jobe's second, self-titled album, released on 10 February 2009. It was later released by Phillips, Craig and Dean on 5 May 2009 as the lead single from their album, Fearless. Their version quickly gained popularity, reaching No. 1 on Billboard's Hot Christian Songs chart in August 2009.
