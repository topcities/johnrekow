Phil Wickham continues to release good and popular songs.

As of March of 2026,
Christian contemporary and worship musician Phil Wickham
has released eleven studio albums, four live albums, and over 43 singles.

One single he released in 2025 was, Homesick For Heaven.

"Homesick for Heaven" peaked at No. 14 on Billboard's Hot Christian Songs,
and No. 12 on the Digital Song Sales.

As of March of 2026, Homesick for Heaven continued to be
one of the top 10 most popular Christian songs
according to 20 The Countdown Magazine.


Homesick For Heaven is found on Phil Wickham's album, Song of the Saints.

Song of the Saints is the tenth studio album
by American contemporary Christian singer Phil Wickham.

The regular edition of this album was released on 12 September 2025.

On 13 March 2026,
a deluxe edition of the album was scheduled to be released.
The deluxe edition is to feature guest appearances from Crowder,
Lauren Daigle, Brandon Lake, CAIN, Jamie MacDonald, Elevation Worship, Michael W. Smith, and Chris Tomlin.
