Gideon Prayer


The Lord, Yaw-Who-Wah had blessed me for many years with an amazing and loving family.
One of our many favourite family moments, was when we spent a week going on holiday, in July of 2020, by enjoying a week along Oregon's sea side.
The area is known as the Central Oregon Coast, and the week was very blessed, with many loving and peaceful memories and moments.

It was almost strange how we got along so well, all week long. There were, of course, walks along the beach, the fresh ocean wind, the very bright, deep blue ocean waves, the continual roar of the sea, looking out at the Pacific Ocean as the sun would set, the seagulls, and going for walks in places that had little shops of souvenirs, and gifts of odds and ends.

Each morning, we would enjoy a big, iced coffee with chocolate and caramel mixed in, and on one day, my wife and I were watching my daughter have a go at surfing down a big sand dune, we were all eating exotic flavours of ice cream, enjoying dozens of flavours of salt water taffy, having the freedom of driving and travelling in our van, as we pleased, and discovering hidden, old roads, with beautiful, purple flowers growing just right there and no place else.

The scenes, the ocean views, the moments and the memories, were beautifully blessed and protected with massive amounts of love and peace.

The Bible study we are about to begin, here, next, reminds me of this special time.

One day, as we were exploring some small shops along the highway, we found ourselves in the area of downtown Newport, Oregon. There was a used bookshop there, and so we popped in for a quick browse.

I found a sparkly gold, hardcover Gideon's Bible.
There was handwriting inside that indicated that it used to be in one of the hotel rooms at the Waldport Hotel. The city of Waldport is about 15 miles south of Newport, Oregon.

There was an interesting study in the front of this Bible, and so, I seem to recall that they gave it to me for free, and by looking at the material found in this Bible, we now have the Bible study that we are about to begin right now.

So, even though it is mainly just a good Bible study to enjoy here, this indirectly reminds me, personally, of that special week of being with my family, enjoying the Oregon Coast in July of 2020. In fact, many of these Bible studies are inspired by people and events of things learnt by the people and the situations that we have gone through over the years.

Let's learn a little bit more about Gideons International.
From Wikipedia, the free encyclopaedia
Gideons International is an evangelical Christian association founded in 1899 in Wisconsin. The Gideons' primary activity is distributing copies of the Bible free of charge.
The association is most widely known for its Bibles placed in lodging rooms. The Gideons also distribute to hospitals and other medical offices, schools and colleges, military bases, as well as jails and prisons. The association takes its name from the Biblical figure Gideon depicted in the book of Judges 6.

The Gideons began distributing free Bibles in 1908.
As of July 2020, the headquarters of Gideons International is in Nashville, Tennessee.

That information comes from Wikipedia.


Many of the common places of lodging in the United States are known as motels and hotels. Traditionally, in the side table drawer next to the bed would be a hardcover Bible book. It contained the NKJV of the Bible without any extra notes or footnotes.
Many motels and hotels might still have this Bible in their rooms today.
