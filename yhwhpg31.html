Commentary: Many pastors of many churches and mega-churches may have, at one time or another,
said something to this effect...

The brothers are now going to come forward and collect the tithes and offerings.

That might sound good, but is it really true?

When someone says something that sounds true and good, we can search it out in the Bible, and we will likely come to one of three possible conclusions...
1. It matches what the Bible says, and so it is true. Bible verses say it and support it and agree with what was said.
2. It is not found in the Bible, which may indicate that it may not be true, and may, most likely be not true.

there are Bible verses that say the opposite of that, and they are in direct opposition to what someone said,
