Other Oregon Cameras


Lincoln City and Newport are two small cities along the west border of Oregon, next to the Pacific Ocean.
From Portland, Oregon, heading in a south west direction, Lincoln City is 87 miles away.
Also going in a similar south west direction, the distance from Portland, Oregon to Newport is 131 miles.
The distance from Lincoln City, going south, to Newport, Oregon, is 25 miles.
