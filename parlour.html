FYI: Secret Webpages Discuss Alternate Movie Versions
Step 1. Go to imdb.com, and navigate to a specific movie title.
Step 2. Look at the URL. Remove the question mark and all characters to the right of the question mark.
After the title number (tt number), there is a forward-slash.
Step 3. After the last forward-slash, add this to the end of the URL:
alternateversions

Here is an example using the above steps with the 1894 silent film short, "Carmencita":

Step 1. http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0000001/?ref_=fn_al_tt_3

Step 2. http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0000001/

Step 3. http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0000001/alternateversions

There should only be a single forward-slash before the alternateversions.
