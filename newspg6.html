California City Distances
Near the middle area of California, going from north to south, is Sacramento, San Francisco,
and then San Jose.
Sacramento, going south west to San Francisco is 88 miles with tolls, or 95 miles without tolls.
San Francisco, going south east to San Jose is about 48 miles away.
Sacramento to San Jose is 120 miles.

Near the south end of the state is Los Angeles.
San Francisco to Los Angeles is 383 miles with tolls, or 388 miles without tolls.
Sacramento to Los Angeles is 386 miles.

South of Los Angeles, at the south west corner of Califonia is San Diego.
Los Angeles, going south to San Diego is 120 miles.

Disneyland is in Anaheim, which is in Orange County, California.
The distance from Los Angeles, going south to Disneyland in Anaheim is 26 miles.
