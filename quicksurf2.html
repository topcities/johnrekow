Force Google to go to a Specific Country using No Country Redirect (NCR)
Here are two examples:
USA:  https://www.google.com/ncr?gws_rd=ssl#num=100&gl=us&noj=1&q=
UK:  https://www.google.co.uk/ncr?gws_rd=ssl#num=100&gl=uk&noj=1&q=

Does your VPN pass the Google Test?
Google is able to determine your approximate geographic location EVEN THROUGH VPNs!
To test this, go to Google News UK ( https://news.google.co.uk/ ) and look at the news in the upper right column.
Are those stories close to where you are?
For maximum privacy, do not use Google.
Google's VPN circumvention does not work on connections using the Tor Browser.
However, Google may sometimes block your ability to use their site if you try to visit them using the Tor Browser.
