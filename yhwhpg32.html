Although they each have their imperfections and their own issues with some areas of translation precision,
The King James Version (KJV) (1st Edition, released in the year 1611) tells us what it SAYS in English,
The New Living Translation (NLT) (5th Edition, 2015) tells us what is MEANS in English,
And The Scriptures 2009 (TS2009) (3rd Edition, 2015) helps us pronounce the names in Hebrew whilst reading the Bible in English.
