People that enjoy spreading gossip are known as talebearers or whisperers.
The Bible teaches us that, one of the ways how we can help to control our tongue, is to avoid talebearing, being a tale-bearer, and to avoid the spreading of gossip and rumours.
In other words, avoid judging other people that are not there, with you, and avoid talking about someone if that person is not there, in the same room with you at that time.

This big study that we just did in the book of Joshua 22 shows us what happens when we talk about someone, or a group of people, and we do not confront them about it directly.
